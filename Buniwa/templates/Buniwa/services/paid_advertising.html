Google Ads Pay-Per-Click (PPC)

Google Ads Pay-Per-Click (PPC) is a powerful online advertising model where advertisers pay a fee each time their ad is clicked. This method allows businesses to gain immediate visibility on Google's search results page, driving targeted traffic to their websites. For small businesses in Kenya, Google Ads PPC offers a cost-effective way to reach potential customers quickly and efficiently. At Buniwa Creations, we specialize in creating and managing PPC campaigns that deliver high ROI and meet your business goals.

Why Your Business Needs Google Ads PPC

  • Immediate Results

    Unlike SEO, which can take time to show results, Google Ads PPC provides immediate visibility on search engine results pages, driving instant traffic to your website.

  • Targeted Advertising

    PPC allows you to target specific keywords, demographics, locations, and even the time of day your ads are shown, ensuring you reach the right audience at the right time.

  • Cost Control

    With PPC, you only pay when someone clicks on your ad, making it a cost-effective advertising method. You can also set daily budgets to control your ad spend.

  • Measurable Results

    PPC campaigns provide detailed analytics, allowing you to track clicks, impressions, conversions, and other key metrics. This data helps you measure the effectiveness of your campaigns and make informed decisions.

MEASURING SUCCESS

We measure success in Google Ads PPC through various key performance indicators (KPIs), including:

Click-Through Rate (CTR)

The percentage of people who click on your ad after seeing it. A high CTR indicates that your ad is relevant and compelling.

Conversion Rate

The percentage of visitors who take the desired action on your landing page, such as making a purchase or filling out a form. This measures the effectiveness of your ad and landing page.

Cost Per Click (CPC)

The average amount you pay for each click on your ad. Lower CPCs help you get more clicks within your budget.

Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)

The revenue generated for every dollar spent on your PPC campaign. This metric helps you assess the profitability of your campaigns.
